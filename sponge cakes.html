Sponge cake recipe

A sponge cake is a very light and simple cake whose main ingredients are mainly flour and eggs. Specifically what seperate a sponge cake from the everyday cake is the absence of milk in the recipe. One would think it would have a very hard texture because it has no milk but I beg to differ. It is the fluffiest cake you will ever taste and it is very good for the vegetarians and also and most imoportantly, the lactose intolerant. I realy prefer it to the traditional vanilla caeke because there is somethimg about its texture and also how light, fluffy and simple it is. I also really love making it it always comes out perfect. Look how apetising and fluffy it is below. I dare you to try it for it is amazingly tasty.

  • Eggs 200ml🍰

    • Ingredients

    • Eggs 200ml🍰Water 200ml
    • Flour 250g
    • Cornflour 25g
    • White sugar 200g🎂
    • Baking powder 20g🍥
    • overlet 30ml🥮
    • 1/4 teaspoon of salt🍰

    Directions

    1. Carefully weigh all th dry ingredients into a bowl
    2. Sift the dry ingredients mixture into the mixing ball
    3. Add the rest of the ingredients into the mixing bowl
    4. Use any type of mixer until dough is fluffy and has attained a white colour
    5. Add the mixture into the bakimg tray of your choice
    6. Bake at 200 degrees celcius for 45 minutes

    This tasty cake should taste like a soft vanilla cake. It is like in the above pictures cream in colour with a nice golden brown top. It should definitely smell fresh.
